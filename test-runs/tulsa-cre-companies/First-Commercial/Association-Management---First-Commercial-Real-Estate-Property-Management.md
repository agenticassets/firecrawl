Association management
----------------------

First Commercial is pleased to offer [management services in Tulsa](https://www.fcasok.com/)
 for Home Owner’s Associations, Condos, Single Family Homes and Duplexes. It is our belief that a property should be maintained in the best condition possible within a set budget. For HOAs, we provide monthly reports, as well as access to an Association Portal which provides a real-time look at the full accounting systems, bank balances, work orders and budgets.

When you choose First Commercial to manage your Association, you not only get a qualified team of licensed property managers, you also get the benefit of a company with over 30 years of multifamily experience. In addition to Association Management, Owners can choose management of their unit at a discounted rate which includes leasing, marketing and  maintenance.
