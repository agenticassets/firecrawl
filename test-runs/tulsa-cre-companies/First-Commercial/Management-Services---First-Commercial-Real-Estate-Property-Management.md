Multifamily Management
----------------------

First Commercial Multifamily Management prides itself in communication with Owners. One of the many benefits you will enjoy is having an asset manager assigned to your property. Your asset manager will review reports monthly, as will our on-staff CPA. 

We provide quarterly inspections of all units to the condition is being maintained as well as to ensure ensure smoke detectors are operational and filters are changed.   
  

As a management company, our biggest focus is on increasing NOI. We have an impressive history of  increasing rents and finding ways to creatively add to the income of a property.

**By providing exceptional service to our Owners, we:**

*   Maximize NOI
*   Negotiate Leases
*   Provide Planning and Budgeting
*   Maintain Property Appearance
*   Provide Advertising and Cross Marketing
*   Utilize Resident Screening
*   Focus on Resident Retention
*   Provide Monthly Reporting: Via Email or Online
*   Encourage Direct Deposit / 24-hour payments
*   Leasing by Professional Brokers
*   Perform Preventative Maintenance
