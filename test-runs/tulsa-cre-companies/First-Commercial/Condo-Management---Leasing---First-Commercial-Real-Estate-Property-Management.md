Home & condo management
-----------------------

First Commercial offers professional management and leasing services to Owners of condo units and single family investment properties. 

Your investment will be in good hands with our licensed real estate property managers. We will handle the marketing of your unit or single family home and then run comprehensive credit and background screenings on qualified applicants.  We address all maintenance issues in a timely manner, day or night, and you will be kept up to date with monthly reports.

Our Management & Leasing Services Include:

*   Screen Residents
*   Negotiate Leases
*   Professional Photography & Marketing
*   Monthly Reporting

*   Rent Collection
*   24 Hour Maintenance
*   Quarterly Pest Control
*   Quarterly Property Inspections
