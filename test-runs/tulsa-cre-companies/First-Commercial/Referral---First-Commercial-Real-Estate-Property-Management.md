Why First Commercial?
---------------------

Why take your valuable time learning the multifamily market, properties, financial analysis, property inspections, condition, financing, and the unique negotiating skills specific to multifamily acquisitions or dispositions? Just sit back, let us do the work and utilize over 30 years of our multifamily experience, to provide you with additional revenue.

It is the desire of First Commercial Real Estate Services Corporation to provide services to the Brokerage Community by offering multiple options under which First Commercial will accept referrals.

[Contact Us](mailto:info@first-commercial.com)

It is our belief that full disclosure, superior communication and service between the parties can build lasting relationships. We encourage and embrace referrals from the brokerage community and want to work with other companies and brokers while providing the highest quality of service for the benefit of their Clients and Customers. We specialize in all types of multifamily properties and therefore have the knowledge, experience, contacts, negotiating skills and processes to best assist a Client with their multifamily needs.
