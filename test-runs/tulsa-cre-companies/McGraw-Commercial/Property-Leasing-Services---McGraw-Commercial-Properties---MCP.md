### Tenant  
Representation

[Learn More](https://www.mcgrawcp.com/services-tenant-representation)

Real estate is a dynamic and complicated industry. There are so many industry-related terms and market details that it’s almost impossible for someone not actively involved with real estate to know everything.  
  
MCP is committed to finding and completing a lease at the best property for you. MCP’s tenant representation service provides tenants with unbiased advice and advocacy during the leasing process, allowing you to focus on your business. As your representative, we advocate for you and provide expert services and current market information to assist you in making a sound business decision regarding your space requirements, location and lease terms.
