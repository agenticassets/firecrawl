About MCP
=========

We’re a privately-owned, full-service commercial real estate firm offering unmatched local market knowledge and unwavering loyalty and commitment.

[Work With Us](https://www.mcgrawcp.com/contact)

Founded in Tulsa in 2008, we’ve built a reputation for delivering expertise, integrity, and results. Whether you’re buying, selling, or leasing, we approach every transaction with the care and commitment we offer family and friends.  
  
With over 200 years of combined experience and $1 billion in successful deals, our brokers bring firsthand knowledge of the market—and the process. Many of us have walked in your shoes, navigating the same challenges as investors and property owners. That’s why we prioritize your goals, offering honest advice and a personalized approach to simplify even the most complex decisions.  
  
Our flat management structure empowers us to act quickly and adapt to market changes without the bureaucracy of larger firms. Our in-house marketing and research teams create tailored strategies, high-quality materials, and insightful market trend reports, ensuring your property or investment opportunity is positioned to succeed.  
  
MCP thrives on a collaborative, entrepreneurial spirit that sets us apart. We’re not just a team—we’re a "we" culture. Our brokers and staff work together seamlessly, mentoring and supporting one another to deliver the best outcomes for our clients. When you work with one of us, you get the collective power of all of us.
