Lonnie Trecek
-------------

Sales Associate

Lonnie Trecek Graduated of Clark School of business with a degree small business. He as been involved in many facets of real estate, including commercial and residential sales, construction and lending since 1970. Lonnie and his family owned and operated a Real Estate office, and owned a Mortgage company and provided FHA and conventional financing along with Commercial financing.

His focus has been on commercial office sales and leasing, as well as commercial financing. He is a member of the national Vietnam veterans of America; he also served on the national and state level of the Oklahoma Mortgage Broker Association and held the office of the vice president and the president for the state of Oklahoma Mortgage Brokers Association.
